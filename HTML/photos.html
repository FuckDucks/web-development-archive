  • Leo

    Leo represents a lion. It is easy to spot in the spring and has a shape that looks like a backward question mark.

  • Orion

    Orion is one of the most famous constellations. It looks like a hunter and is easy to find because of the three bright stars in a row called Orion’s Belt.

  • The Big Dipper

    The Big Dipper is a famous group of seven bright stars in the northern sky. It is part of the constellation Ursa Major, which means “Great Bear” in Latin. The Big Dipper has been used for navigation for a long time because it helps people find the North Star, Polaris. Many cultures see different shapes and stories in these seven stars. In Korean tradition, Bukduchilseong is believed to have a special spiritual meaning related to life and destiny.
